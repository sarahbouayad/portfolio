About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Sarah Bouayad

Growing up in Morocco, I was passionate about advocating for my community, so at the age of 19, I packed my bags and moved across the world seeking a career in human rights. After that, I worked in the non-profit sector, but found myself leaning more towards creative projects. Eventually, I enrolled in an UX course and that opened the world of tech.

My journey to programming has prepared me for a life of continuous learning, and navigating through ambiguity with perseverance. As a creative professional, I've helped clients amplify their voices, redesigned their brand, and launched beautiful and engaging websites. As a resourceful software engineer who thrives in collaborative atmospheres, I am confident I will be a valuable addition to your engineering team.
